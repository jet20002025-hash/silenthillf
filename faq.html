FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about Silent Hill f

General Questions

When will Silent Hill f be released?

Silent Hill f is scheduled to release on September 25, 2025, for PlayStation 5, Windows, and Xbox Series X/S. Early access begins September 23, 2025, for Deluxe Edition pre-orders.

How much does Silent Hill f cost?

The Standard Edition is priced at $69.99 USD, Deluxe Edition at $89.99 USD, and Day One Edition at $79.99 USD. Pre-orders are currently available on all platforms.

What makes Silent Hill f different from other Silent Hill games?

Silent Hill f is set in 1960s Japan with a completely new storyline and Japanese cultural elements, featuring melee-only combat and a female protagonist named Hinako Shimizu.

Can I use firearms in Silent Hill f?

No, Silent Hill f focuses entirely on melee combat using weapons like pipes, baseball bats, and hammers that have durability and will break over time.

How long does it take to complete Silent Hill f?

The main story takes approximately 12-15 hours to complete, with additional time needed for multiple endings and collectibles.

Does Silent Hill f have multiple difficulty settings?

The game is designed with a challenging difficulty level and doesn't offer traditional easy modes, focusing on survival horror experience.

Who wrote the story for Silent Hill f?

The story was written by Ryukishi07, a renowned Japanese suspense writer, with music composed by original Silent Hill composer Akira Yamaoka.

Can I play Silent Hill f without playing previous games?

Yes, Silent Hill f features a completely new story and characters, making it accessible to newcomers while maintaining series DNA.

Technical Questions

What platforms will Silent Hill f be available on?

Silent Hill f will be available on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, and PC (Windows) via Steam, Epic Games Store, and Microsoft Store.

Does the game support ray tracing?

Yes, Silent Hill f supports ray tracing on compatible hardware, enhancing the atmospheric lighting and visual fidelity.

Is there mod support for PC?

While mod support hasn't been officially confirmed, the PC version may support community mods post-launch.

Does the game support ultra-wide monitors?

Yes, Silent Hill f supports ultra-wide monitor resolutions on PC for an enhanced immersive experience.

Story & Gameplay Questions

What is the Otherworld in Silent Hill f?

The Otherworld is a supernatural dimension with Japanese shrine aesthetics that players can switch to, offering different challenges and visual design.

Are there collectibles and side content?

Yes, the game features numerous collectibles, consumables, and puzzles that provide deeper insight into Japanese culture and the game's lore.

What themes does Silent Hill f explore?

The game explores themes of social pressure, gender discrimination, bullying, psychological trauma, and identity through a teenage girl's perspective.

Does the game have jump scares?

Silent Hill f focuses more on psychological horror and atmospheric tension rather than traditional jump scares, though some intense moments may startle players.
