About Silent Hill f

Experience the Latest Silent Hill Chapter

Silent Hill f is the newest installment in Konami's acclaimed psychological horror series. Developed by NeoBards Entertainment and powered by Unreal Engine 5, this game transports players to 1960s Japan for a deeply atmospheric and emotionally intense experience.

Set in the fictional town of Ebisugaoka, players follow Hinako Shimizu, a high school student navigating a fog-consumed town while solving puzzles and fighting grotesque monsters. The game explores the Japanese horror concept of "finding terror in beauty."

What Does the "f" Mean?

Konami has kept the meaning of the letter "f" intentionally ambiguous, allowing for multiple interpretations:
